About

A pure coding arena, to test out your skills of problem solving in real time. Compete with and among your peers, to confirm the right to 'The Code-Throne', and overcome your flaws with practice, because in this game no one loses!

Rules And Guidelines

1. Registration fee Rs. 50/-per student which is to be paid in cash at registration desk on 2nd September.

2. This is a team event. Maximum of 2 members are allowed per team.

3. Code copying will be strictly compiled and will not be entertained. The defaulter will be disqualified at the moment.

4. The venue for the competition: Azim PremJi Block (M-Block), CSE Dept., MIET Meerut.

5. All the participants are required to carry their college ID CARDS (no entries will be accepted without College ID Cards).
